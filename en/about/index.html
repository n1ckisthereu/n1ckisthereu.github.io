Hi my name Nicholas and as long as I can remember by “people” I’ve always had an immense I wanted to know how it all worked, well I started very early, at 8 years old, I always really liked to play I had my first experiences with famous people consoles, my hacking saga started with the game “God of War” where I discovered the speedruns and researching by chance I ended up in a reverse engineering video on consoles, well I didn’t have a PC, so I went to Lan Houses a lot but I couldn’t do a lot of things access to lan’s pc’s were very limited, so i would go home from family members and that’s where I started unlocking consoles. Well I didn’t have the money to buy a PC, the city where I lived was very small and for not having much technical knowledge in the area of ​​informatics in the city, for whatever reason defect they discarded the machines (mainly in city halls) so I went to searches for these computers, I started to gather and assemble many machines, but the machines they didn’t have very powerful hardware, so I couldn’t use these machines to play games they didn’t perform very well, windows choked so much it was impossible to use! And it was my gateway to the Linux world, well I remember the first distribution that I used was Slack with the XFCE interface, I didn’t spend a lot of time in slack and went for ArchLinux, and I took a love for that distro and it was my system until the year 2021, good in all these transitions i discovered the programming, i started doing some automations with shell script.

From that moment I had an insatiable thirst for knowledge, and from that moment from then on I started to devour everything, I always loved reading so I spent hours and hours consuming different types of content, well I’ve traced a long way here, which I won’t detail here so it doesn’t become a book.

Now 18 years old, I work as a Pentester and developer, and I decided to create this blog to share my knowledge, show my learning, learn from you, and to show a little more about the world of technology and hacking, hope you like it 😊!
